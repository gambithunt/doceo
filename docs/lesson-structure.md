# 🧠 Deep Learning Loop — Lesson Framework

A structured approach to teaching that moves a learner from curiosity → understanding → application → mastery.

---

## 1. 🎯 Orientation (Why should I care?)
**Goal:** Create relevance and curiosity

- What is this about?
- Why does it matter in the real world?
- Where will this be used?

> Keep it short. Hook, don’t overload.

---

## 2. 🧩 Mental Model (Big Picture First)
**Goal:** Provide a scaffold for understanding

- Explain the overall shape of the concept
- Use analogy, visualization, or relationships
- Show how things connect before diving into rules

> If they can picture it, they can retain it

---

## 3. 🧠 Core Concepts (The Rules)
**Goal:** Build true understanding

- Define key ideas clearly
- Show relationships between concepts
- Limit to 3–5 core ideas per lesson

---

## 4. 🔧 Guided Construction (How to Think)
**Goal:** Teach reasoning, not just facts

- Walk through the process step-by-step
- Narrate decision-making
- Highlight key thinking moments

---

## 5. 👀 Worked Example (See It in Action)
**Goal:** Bridge theory to reality

- Solve a real example
- Show every step
- Explain why each step happens

---

## 6. 🧪 Active Practice (Do It Yourself)
**Goal:** Convert understanding into ability

- Provide a similar problem
- Let the student attempt first
- Then show the solution

> Learning happens when effort is required

---

## 7. 🔁 Feedback Loop (Correct & Refine)
**Goal:** Prevent incorrect understanding

- Show common mistakes
- Explain why they happen
- Provide corrections

---

## 8. 🧠 Retrieval & Reinforcement
**Goal:** Strengthen memory

- Ask recall questions (without notes)
- Encourage explaining the concept back
- Quick recap prompts

> Retrieval is more powerful than re-reading

---

## 9. 🌍 Transfer (Apply in New Contexts)
**Goal:** Ensure deep understanding

- Introduce variations of the concept
- Apply it in different scenarios
- Slightly twist the problem

---

## 10. 🧾 Summary (Compression)
**Goal:** Create a mental cheat sheet

- 3–5 key takeaways
- One clear mental model
