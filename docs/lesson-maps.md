# Lesson Framework → AI Mode → Model Tier

## 1. Orientation
- AI mode: `tutor` or `subject-hints`
- Tier: `fast` for simple intros, `default` if personalized

## 2. Mental Model
- AI mode: `lesson-plan` for planning
- AI mode: `tutor` for delivery
- Tier: `thinking` to design, `default` to explain

## 3. Core Concepts
- AI mode: `lesson-plan` for structure
- AI mode: `tutor` for delivery
- Tier: `thinking` to choose and order concepts, `default` to teach them

## 4. Guided Construction
- AI mode: `lesson-plan` for step design
- AI mode: `tutor` or `lesson-chat` for interactive walkthrough
- Tier: `thinking` to build, `default` to run live

## 5. Worked Example
- AI mode: `lesson-plan`
- AI mode: `lesson-chat`
- Tier: `thinking` to generate strong examples, `default` to explain them conversationally

## 6. Active Practice
- AI mode: `lesson-chat`
- Tier: `default`

## 7. Feedback Loop
- AI mode: `lesson-chat` or `tutor`
- Tier: `default`

## 8. Retrieval & Reinforcement
- AI mode: `lesson-chat`
- Tier: `default` or `fast` for simple quiz prompts

## 9. Transfer
- AI mode: `lesson-chat`
- Tier: `default`, sometimes `thinking` for complex transfer problems

## 10. Summary
- AI mode: `lesson-chat` or `tutor`
- Tier: `fast` or `default`
